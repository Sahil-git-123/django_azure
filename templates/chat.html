Glossary & Help Page

Glossary

    Market Scope

  • World(Top 32) is the aggregation of Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, China, Colombia, Ecuador, Egypt, France, Germany, Great Britain, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Morocco, Nigeria, Pakistan, Peru, Philippines, Poland, Romania, Russian Federation, South Africa, South Korea, Spain, Thailand, Turkey, United States & Vietnam
  • EMEA(Top 40) OG is the aggregation of Algeria, Belgium, Egypt, France, Germany, Great Britain, Italy, Morocco, Netherlands, Nigeria, Pakistan, Poland, Romania, Russian Federation, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Spain & Turkey
  • ASEAN BU is the aggregation of Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand & Vietnam
  • CEE BU is the aggregation of Italy, Poland, Romania & Russian Federation
  • Mexico BU consists of Mexico
  • USA BU consists of USA

    • *OG- Operating Group, BU- Business Unit

    Time-Period

    Data shown from Sep 2016 - Aug 2018 in Jiffy

    YTD (Year-To-Date e.g. YTD Feb would be the cumulative sum of Jan & Feb month)
    • Can ask Jiffy for Share % & Mix % starting from Jan 2017.
    • Growth % & Share Change can be viewed from Jan 2018 - Aug 2018 (8 periods only)
    MTD (Month-To-Date i.e. Current Month)
    • Growth % & Share Change can be viewed from Sep 2017 to Aug 2018 (12 Periods)
    • Share % & Mix % can be viewed for 24 months.

    Beverage Category Definition

  • NARTD = Sum of SSD, Energy Drinks, Juices, VAD, PBB, Waters, Sports Drinks, RTD Tea & RTD Coffee
  • SSD = Sparkling Soft Drinks or also called as CSD or CSp
  • Waters = Sum of Plain Packaged Water, Flavored Water & Enhanced Water Beverages. Excludes Bulk (10L & above) & Pouch Water
Help

    Question Format

  • KPI(Mandatory) + Dimensions + Time-Period.

    Best to try with

  • Best to try with any KPI, Manufacturer or Time-Period

    Dimensions

  • You can also have single or all categories as part of your question
  • You can dynamically reduce the bar in chart by cancelling out the bar legends. This will help in viewing the cluttered chart.

    KPI

  • Always remember to mention the KPI, it is mandatory

    Chart type

  • You can also mention the type of chart you would prefer, and if possible, the response will be generated using the same. (bar = Vertical bar chart, barh = Horizontal bar chart or line chart)

    Time-Period with KPI

  • KPI should match with time-period e.g. YTD value growth won't give answer if asked in 2017
  • User can ask question with time-period using specific months or trend or last year or current year or between the months
